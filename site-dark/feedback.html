What I changed in response

The feedback drove three concrete revisions to the chassis design:

1

Reinforced center plate

Added internal ribs and bumped infill from 25% to 40% gyroid where users reported flex. Plate stiffness improved measurably (see Evidence).

Driven by: feedback #1 [Replace]

2

Deeper motor screw bosses

Extended the motor mount hole depth from 6 mm to 9 mm so M3 screws fully thread without bottoming.

Driven by: feedback #3 [Replace]

3

Front-arrow indicator

Embossed an arrow into the top of the front-right arm so orientation is obvious at a glance, even in poor light.

Driven by: feedback #4 [Replace]

Design iterations

Three rounds of design revision. v3 is what's printed and flying today.

The actual tests 🧪

💥

Drop test

Dropped from 1.5 m onto concrete five times. v3 frame: zero cracks. v2: cracked one arm on the third drop.

⏱️

Flight time

5 min 40 sec hover on a 4S 1500 mAh — within 8% of the same components on a stock carbon frame.

📡

Vibration

Gyro noise stayed below default Betaflight filter cutoffs — printed frame doesn't add bad vibration.

🔄

Real crash recovery

After an actual crash, replacing the broken arm took 22 minutes total. Faster than ordering a part.

Did it solve the problem? 🎯

💸 Cost: Frame cost dropped from ~$65 to ~$4. ✓

🛡️ Fragility: PLA+ flexes and absorbs impact instead of shattering. ✓

🔧 Repairability: Single-arm reprint in <90 min vs replacing whole frame. ✓

🎨 Customization: Parametric STL — anyone with Fusion 360 can tweak it. ✓

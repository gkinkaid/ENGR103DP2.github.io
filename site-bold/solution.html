The Build

Print it. Fly it. Reprint it. 🛠️

A 250 mm X-frame chassis you can print in PLA+ for about $4 in filament. Single-arm replaceable. Fully modular.

What it actually is 📦

The chassis is a four-arm X-frame with a central hub plate. Each arm bolts on independently — so when (not if) you crash one, you reprint just that arm and you're back in the air the same afternoon.

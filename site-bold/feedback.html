What I changed 🔧

Every piece of feedback drove a real change to the design.

1

Reinforced center plate

Internal ribs added + infill bumped from 25% → 40% gyroid. Killed the flex users felt when tightening the battery strap.

2

Deeper screw bosses

Motor mount holes extended from 6 mm to 9 mm so M3 screws thread fully without bottoming out.

3

Front-arrow indicator

Embossed arrow on the front-right arm. You can see it AND feel it — works in low light too.

How it evolved 📈

Three iterations. v3 is what's printed and flying right now.

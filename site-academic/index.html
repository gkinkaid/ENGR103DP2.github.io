How an Inexpensive Drone Can
Improve Police Departments

Riley Merry & Gavin Kinkaid · University of San Diego

Problem Statement

Police officers often risk their lives operating in urban structures. A drone can eliminate these risks. Design a device an officer can operate with their base-level training and is cost-effective so it can be community-supported for funding. The device should be lightweight and easily expendable in case of damage.

1. The Need

Cost

Department budgets cannot absorb $10k+ tactical robots, especially for high-risk deployments.

Expendable

The unit must be cheap enough to lose without hesitation.

Intuitive

Operable with base-level officer training — no specialist required.

Visual feedback

Live camera so officers can see the room before entering it.
